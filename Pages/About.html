ABOUT US:

OUR VALUES:

Professionalism, trust, integrity and fairness We believe in delivering our services in a professional manner, to ensure that we win the trust of our clients. We also ascribe to conducting business with integrity & fairness. Respect & dignity At HG we embrace with respect the differences in beliefs and the diversity in our country, we thus look to conduct our services in a way that brings dignity to all stakeholders. Accountability We believe in taking accountability for clients/ stakeholder satisfaction. This is reflected in our work ethics policy, and corrective measures protocols. Excellence We strive for excellence in all areas of our work. Our aim is to continuously find innovative solution to enhance our performance.

OUR HISTORY:

Hundzukani Group (Pty) Ltd is a Construction Project Management & Advisory firm established in 2017. We provide turn-key construction project management services, cost consulting and advisory for the engineering & built environment sector. We are well-resourced to service both public and private sector clients.

OUR MISSION:

Our aim is to offer cutting edge construction consulting and building services, using our plethora of skills, expertise & technology. To offer quality cost effective construction solutions, that support environmental sustainability for future generations. To make Hundzukani Group the consultancy of choice by using our detailed knowledge of construction, skills and professional expertise to deliver a practical and commercial cost management approach to all construction projects. This will be achieved by keeping updates with the latest technology, regulations and market trends.

OUR VISION:

Our vision is to offer innovative construction services to stakeholders across the African continent, to play a role in the development of the infrastructure economy and support its long- term sustainability goals. To provide innovative and highly professional customised services to all our clients and the community through ways that promote the development of previously disadvantaged communities.
